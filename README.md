# ⚡ ChargeOverSpeed ⚡

**The ultimate Magisk module to unlock your device's charging potential.**

`ChargeOverSpeed` is a powerful Magisk module designed to optimize and accelerate your charging speed by adjusting thermal throttling limits.

---

## 🚀 Editions

Choose the version that fits your needs:

*   **🔥 Hardcore Edition**  
    No thermal limits. Maximum speed, maximum heat. *Use with caution.*
    
*   **🍃 Green Edition**  
    Optimized speed with safety in mind. Includes a battery thermal limit to protect your battery longevity.

---

## 🛠 Installation

1. Download the latest `.zip` from the **[Releases Page](https://github.com/HongZhangLim/ChargeOverSpeed/releases/latest)**.
2. Open the **Magisk App**.
3. Go to the **Modules** tab.
4. Tap **Install from storage** and select the zip file.
5. **Reboot** your device to apply changes.

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

**Use at your own risk.** Removing thermal limits can cause your device to run extremely hot. The developer is not responsible for hardware damage, degraded battery health, or any other issues caused by the use of this module, especially the **Hardcore Edition**.
